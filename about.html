Who We Are?

We are a group of passionate and driven 4th-year students from ACLC College of Taytay, dedicated to creating innovative solutions through our thesis project. Our team, consisting of Elyssa Jane Caro, Denmark Cruz S., Dacut Kinnet E., and Telan Matthew Leigh, has come together to develop a Web-based Interactive Map and Directory with Rating and Analytics for CME Original Tiangge, Taytay, Rizal. With a shared commitment to excellence and cutting-edge technology, our project aims to assist customers in easily navigating the stalls while also providing stall owners with a platform to showcase their products and increase visibility. As aspiring professionals, we are excited to bring our knowledge and skills to this project, combining both theory and hands-on experience to create a meaningful and practical solution for the community.
